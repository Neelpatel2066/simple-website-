About Startford Art Youth Festival

The Startford Art Youth Festivalis a vibrant celebration of creativity, talent, and young voices. We bring together aspiring artists, performers, and creators to showcase their work, express themselves, and connect with a wider community.

Our festival is designed to inspire and empower youth through art, music, performance, and innovation. From visual arts and live performances to workshops and interactive sessions, Startford provides a platform where creativity thrives and ideas come to life.

We believe that every young person has a unique story to tell, and through this festival, we aim to give them the space and confidence to share it.

Whether you're an artist, a performer, or someone who simply loves creativity, Startford Art Youth Festival is a place where passion meets opportunity.

Join us as we celebrate the power of youth and the beauty of artistic expression.


Our Mission

To inspire, support, and showcase young creative talent while building a strong and inclusive artistic community.

What to Expect

  • Art exhibitions
  • Live performances
  • Workshops & creative sessions
  • Networking opportunities
  • Competitions and showcases
