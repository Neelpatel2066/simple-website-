STRATFORD YOUTH ART FESTIVAL

 

Workshops at the Stratford Art Youth Festival

The Stratford Art Youth Festival offers a vibrant programme of hands-on workshops designed to inspire creativity, build skills, and connect young artists from diverse backgrounds. Whether you’re a beginner exploring art for the first time or an emerging creator looking to refine your craft, our workshops provide a welcoming and dynamic space to learn and grow.

 

What to Expect

Participants can take part in a wide range of interactive sessions led by experienced artists and educators. Each workshop focuses on creativity, self-expression, and collaboration in a fun and supportive environment.

Workshop Highlights

  • Painting & Drawing – Explore techniques in sketching, watercolor, and acrylics
  • Digital Art & Design – Learn the basics of illustration, animation, and creative software
  • Street Art & Murals – Discover urban art styles and create collaborative pieces
  • Craft & Sculpture – Experiment with materials to bring your ideas to life
  • Performance & Creative Expression – Engage in drama, movement, and storytelling

 

Who Can Join?

Our workshops are open to young people of all skill levels. No prior experience is required—just curiosity and enthusiasm!

Schedule & Booking

Workshops run throughout the festival, with multiple sessions available each day. Spaces may be limited, so we recommend booking in advance to secure your spot.

Why can Join?

  • Learn new artistic skills
  • Meet like-minded young creatives
  • Gain confidence in your abilities
  • Be part of a vibrant artistic community
